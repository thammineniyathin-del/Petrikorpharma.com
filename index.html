Who We Are

Built on Science,
Driven by Purpose

Petrikor Pharma Pvt. Ltd. is a dynamic and innovative pharmaceutical company based in Kolkata, India. Founded by a dedicated group of technocrats and doctors with extensive experience, our company is built on a foundation of scientific excellence, clinical expertise, and an unwavering commitment to improving human health.

From day one, we have moved with purpose. Within our first year, we have established contract manufacturing agreements with three WHO-GMP compliant manufacturers, secured supply contracts across multiple therapeutic categories, and attracted a 40% strategic stake from a UAE-based investment partner — validating both our commercial model and our long-term vision.

Our current focus is branded generics — high-quality, affordable versions of established medicines, delivered under our own brand and supported by a professional sales and medical affairs team. As we grow, our portfolio will expand into injectables, nutraceuticals, intensive healthcare, medical devices, and disposables.

The Story Behind Our Name

Petrichor — The Promise
of New Beginnings

The name “Petrikor” (Petrichor) evokes the distinct, earthy scent that often accompanies the first rain after a long period of dry weather. We chose this name because it symbolises new beginnings, renewal, and the promise of life — a perfect metaphor for our mission to bring fresh, life-enhancing pharmaceutical solutions to the market.

Petrichor
/ ϖet.rı.kôr /
noun — The pleasant, earthy scent produced when rain falls on dry soil. From Greek petra (stone) and ichor (the fluid that flows in the veins of the gods).

For us, it symbolises renewal, hope, and the promise of new life — the very essence of our commitment to healthcare.

Investor Relations

A Partnership Built on
Shared Ambition

Petrikor Pharma is backed by a UAE-based strategic investor holding a 40% equity stake — a partnership that validates our commercial model, accelerates our international ambitions, and positions us strongly for the next phase of growth.

In March 2026, Petrikor Pharma Pvt Ltd entered into a landmark equity partnership with a UAE-based strategic investor, who acquired a 40% stake in the company. This is not a passive financial investment — it is a strategic alliance that opens direct pathways into the GCC pharmaceutical market, the Middle East healthcare sector, and broader international distribution networks.

The partnership reflects confidence in Petrikor's business model, the quality of our manufacturing network, and the strength of our founding team. It provides us with the capital, commercial relationships, and international credibility to execute our vision of becoming a leading pharmaceutical company.

🌍

GCC Market Access

The UAE partnership provides strategic entry points into the Gulf Cooperation Council pharmaceutical market — one of the fastest-growing regions for pharmaceutical imports from India.

💰

Strategic Capital Backing

The 40% equity stake represents meaningful, committed capital that supports our product launch, team building, regulatory investments, and commercial scale-up planned for 2026.

🤝

Commercial & Distribution Synergies

Beyond capital, the partnership creates distribution synergies across healthcare institutions, hospital networks, and pharmaceutical importers in the UAE and wider Middle East region.

📋

India–UAE Trade Framework

Under the India-UAE CEPA (Comprehensive Economic Partnership Agreement), Indian pharmaceutical exports enjoy preferential access to the UAE market — a direct commercial advantage for Petrikor.
